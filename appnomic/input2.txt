Attitudes toward management have also changed.
As my research makes clear,
Gen Y workers see no value in reporting to someone who tracks their work.
What they do value is mentoring and coaching from someone they respect.
Someone, in other words, who is a master-not a general manager.

Now technology itself has become the great general manager.
The classic job of the middle manager will soon disappear.

What does this mean for you?
